# Unresolved customer inputs

Absence never authorizes a default. Required before relevant approval:

## Eligibility and account

- Customer/operator identity, deployment owner, jurisdiction, and confirmation the account may use Binance USD-M Futures and automated trading.
- Intended account/environment and non-secret account-profile identifier.
- API permission/IP/subaccount policy and whether manual/external trading is prohibited while armed.
- Preferred position, margin, and multi-assets modes, subject to authenticated verification.

## Customer policy values

- permitted instruments and symbols/classes;
- maximum account/campaign exposure;
- maximum risk and monetary loss per campaign;
- maximum daily and rolling loss plus rolling window;
- maximum acceptable drawdown;
- maximum customer-permitted leverage;
- minimum required net profit;
- maximum concurrent campaigns;
- permitted sessions/time zone/holiday treatment;
- emergency-close policy;
- production automation authorization;
- data/log retention;
- system-tray and automatic-startup permission.

## Windows, operations, and security

- Supported Windows edition/build, hardware, service/user model, paths, proxy/firewall/EDR, backup destination, recovery objectives, and notification channels.
- Credential custody, rotation, revocation, break-glass, and multi-operator approval requirements.
- Authority to arm, flatten, change policy, approve models/releases, restore backups, or acknowledge incidents.
- Logout/reboot/maintenance behavior for protected components.

## Research and validation

- Historical event data availability/license and retention authorization.
- Required symbols, periods, regimes, and exclusion policy.
- Economic/statistical approval standard and minimum paper/shadow/testnet/controlled-live evidence.
- Model/recalibration approvers, degradation thresholds, and rollback policy.

## Legal/product

- BCEE project ownership/distribution license and open-source policy, including LGPL/MPL handling.
- Compliance acknowledgement text/version and retention.
- Product name/branding, locale, accessibility, display scaling, notifications, and operator workflows.

Until required policy and authorization are complete and validated: `live_trading_enabled=false`.
